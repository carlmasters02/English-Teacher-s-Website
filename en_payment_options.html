Booking & Cancellation Policies

When to Pay

Payment is due before the first lesson of each block or package. Single sessions must be paid at least 24 hours in advance.

Cancellation (by you)

Cancel or reschedule with at least 24 hours' notice for a full reschedule at no cost. Late cancellations (<24 hrs) will forfeit the lesson fee.

Cancellation (by Kady)

In the rare event I need to cancel, I will notify you as soon as possible. The lesson will be rescheduled at a time that suits you, or fully refunded.

Free Trial Lesson

The free 30-minute trial is offered once per student. It is non-transferable and cannot be exchanged for credit.

⏱️ Package Expiry

Lesson block packages are valid for 8 weeks from the purchase date. Unused lessons are not refundable after expiry, but an extension may be granted in special circumstances.

Refunds

Unused lessons within an active package can be refunded on a pro-rata basis. Refund requests must be submitted by email within 7 days of the last attended lesson.
