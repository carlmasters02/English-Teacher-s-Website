Class Options

Choose the class format that best suits your goals, level, and schedule. All classes can be delivered online (Zoom / Google Meet) or in-person in Ho Chi Minh City.

1. One-on-One Private Lessons

🧑 Personalised Learning

Lessons built entirely around your goals, weak areas, and interests. Maximum flexibility in scheduling.

⏱ Session Length

60 or 90 minutes per session. Recommended frequency: 2–3 sessions per week for steady progress.

📊 Levels

Beginner (A1) through Advanced (C1). Free placement assessment included at enrolment.

2. Small Group Classes (2–6 Students)

👥 Collaborative Learning

Practice with peers in a structured yet fun environment. Great for conversation and confidence building.

📅 Fixed Schedule

Morning and evening cohorts available. New groups start on the 1st and 15th of each month.

💰 More Affordable

Group classes are priced lower per student — ideal for those on a budget who still want structured tuition.

3. Business English

💼 Professional Focus

Email writing, presentations, meetings, negotiations, and professional small talk — relevant to your industry.

🏢 Corporate Workshops

Half-day or full-day workshops for teams available on request. Customised content for your company's needs.

✈️ Travel & Hospitality

Specialised modules for professionals in tourism, hospitality, and international trade.

4. Exam Preparation

Exam Target Score Duration Format
IELTS Academic Band 6.0 – 8.0 8–16 weeks Online / In-Person
IELTS General Training Band 5.5 – 7.5 6–12 weeks Online / In-Person
TOEIC (Reading & Listening) 600 – 990 4–8 weeks Online
Cambridge YLE / KET / PET Pass / Pass with Merit 8–12 weeks Online / In-Person

5. Young Learner Classes (Ages 6–15)

🎨 Fun & Interactive

Games, stories, songs, and crafts that make English engaging and memorable for children.

📖 Curriculum Aligned

Support for school English curriculum plus enrichment activities to build confidence beyond the classroom.

👨‍👩‍👧 Parent Updates

Monthly progress reports emailed directly to parents, plus open communication via messaging apps.
