About the Teacher

Qualified, passionate, and dedicated to helping you succeed in English.

Can Tho, Vietnam · 2 Years of Teaching Experience

Hi, I'm Kady!

I'm a freelance Vietnamese English teacher based in Can Tho, Vietnam, with over two years of experience supporting local learners. I hold a degree in English Teaching from Can Tho University and specialize in teaching Grades 6-12.

I believe the key to language learning is confidence - and that comes from a supportive, engaging environment where making mistakes is part of the process. My lessons are warm, structured, and focused on helping students succeed in school English and Vietnam's national exam.

Outside the classroom, I enjoy reading and exploring Can Tho's local culture.

My Teaching Philosophy

Three principles guide every lesson I teach.

❤️

Student-Centered Learning

You are at the heart of every lesson. I listen, adapt, and design sessions around your unique strengths and areas for growth.

🔄

Consistent, Incremental Progress

Language learning is a marathon, not a sprint. I set clear milestones, track your progress, and celebrate every achievement.

😊

Enjoyment & Positivity

Learning a language should be enjoyable. I use humor, interesting topics, and real materials to keep lessons engaging and motivating.
