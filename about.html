About Ms. Linh Nguyen

Ms. Linh
Nguyen

Hello! I'm Linh Nguyen, a passionate freelance English teacher based in Ho Chi Minh City, Vietnam. I hold a Bachelor's degree in English Language Education and a TEFL/TESOL certification, and I have been helping Vietnamese students unlock the power of English for over 8 years.

My teaching philosophy is simple: language learning should be enjoyable, practical, and confidence-building. I create a warm, supportive environment where mistakes are celebrated as stepping stones to fluency.

I specialise in conversational English, business communication, and exam preparation (IELTS, TOEIC, and Cambridge). Whether you are a complete beginner or looking to polish your professional English, I can craft a programme that meets your individual needs.

Teaching Philosophy

I believe that every student learns differently. That's why I take time at the start of our journey together to understand your goals, learning style, and schedule. Lessons are interactive, communicative, and always relevant to real-life situations. I use a blend of communicative language teaching (CLT), task-based learning (TBL), and targeted grammar instruction to ensure you progress steadily and stay motivated.
